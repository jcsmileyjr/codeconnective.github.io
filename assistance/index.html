The bottom line

If you're a meetup, class, hackathon, networking event, etc. that is helping your community for free, we're here to help. After all, our mission is to see people start learning tech and thrive not matter if it's through Code Connector meetup or not.

What we require

Proof

It's not necessary for your group to be a non-profit or anything like that, but we need to see that you've been helping people in you community and at no direct cost.

Plan

Give us a general idea of what you're planning to do and what you'd like to happen. This will give us a great idea of whether our partnership makes sense.

Recognition

Whether in your event description, a banner image, social media posts, or otherwise, it'd helpful and appreciated to be credited as an event sposnor.

Send word our way

Whether you're needing assistance or just want to say hello, we'd love to hear form you.
